Convert Subtitles To Text

You can convert one or multiple SRT file, or one or more ZIP archives containing subtitle files.

The files will be included in alphabetical order, and grouped into chapters (and sub-chapters).

Download

If you own a Kindle, you can select the Kindle friendly format, and send the result as an attachment to your Kindle e-mail address - it will be automatically converted into an e-book you can read on your device.
